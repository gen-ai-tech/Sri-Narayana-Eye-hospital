Our Mission & Vision

At Sri Narayana Eye Hospital & Cornea Centre, our mission is simple: to protect and enhance the gift of sight through innovative medical treatments and a deeply empathetic approach to patient care.

As a leading center for advanced cornea treatments in Tamil Nadu, we bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and accessible healthcare. We are proud to be a women-owned institution that champions inclusivity, ensuring a safe, welcoming, and LGBTQ+ friendly space for everyone.
