Modern Medicine with a Compassionate Touch

At Sri Narayana Eye Hospital & Cornea Centre, our mission is simple: to protect and enhance the gift of sight through innovative medical treatments and a deeply empathetic approach to patient care.

As a leading center for advanced cornea treatments in Tamil Nadu, we bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and accessible healthcare. We are proudly an inclusive, welcoming, and LGBTQ+ friendly space where every patient receives the standard of personalized care they deserve.
